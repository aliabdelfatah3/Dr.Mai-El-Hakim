Meet Your Dermatology & Aesthetics Expert

Consultant Dermatologist & Aesthetic Specialist

Dr. Mai El-Hakim is a highly skilled Consultant Dermatologist & Aesthetic Specialist with over 15 years of experience in advanced skincare and non-surgical aesthetics. She combines medical precision with artistic vision to enhance natural beauty, treat diverse skin conditions, and help clients achieve healthy, radiant, and youthful skin.

Specializing in laser treatments, anti-aging procedures, and skin rejuvenation, Dr. Mai uses the latest technologies and evidence-based techniques to deliver safe, effective, and personalized results. Her compassionate care and commitment to continuous learning ensure exceptional treatments and lasting patient trust.
